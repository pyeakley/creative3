More info


At Bullseye Bakery, we offer custom decorated sugar cookies. All cookies are made to order in our home-based kitchen. Decorated sugar cookies are perfect for gatherings, gifts, and celebrations of all kinds!


Shipping: Under Idaho cottage food laws, I can only ship within the state of Idaho. Any shipping costs are paid for by the customer.


Pricing: Custom orders start at $35 a dozen. Pricing can increase with complicated, time consuming designs or for specialty flavors. Orders must be placed 2 weeks in advance. Any order placed with less notice will incure a rush fee of $10/dozen.


Specialty Flavors:

  • Cookies and cream
  • Snickerdoodle
  • Pumpkin Spice Toffee
